# R2 Expert Revision Task

## Owners

The five specialist profiles revise their own report. The chairman does not rewrite specialist conclusions.

## Inputs

- The profile's R1 report.
- Relevant R1.5 disputes and chairman rulings.
- New Evidence and the current snapshot hash.
- Counter-arguments from other roles that touch this profile's authority.

## Required Delta

- R1 conclusion and R2 conclusion.
- R1 score, R2 score, and score change.
- Changed claim IDs and the Evidence or ruling that caused each change.
- Unchanged contested claims with explicit reason.
- New, removed, and retained conditions and monitoring indicators.
- Remaining `insufficient_evidence` restrictions.

An R2 report that only says it incorporated the chairman's view is invalid. A deterministic synthesis must be labeled fallback and cannot impersonate an expert revision.
